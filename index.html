Breeding

Why

Our goal is to only produce exceptional versatile hunting dogs. We aim to accomplish this by selectively breeding only parents who have proven capabilities. The validation is represented in the titles: VC(Versatile Champion) or UT1(Utility prize 1). We believe if both parents are high performers, the progeny will be to. The ultimate goal of breeding is to progress the breed forward using calculated, smart breedings, then measuring your outcomes using NAVHDA & AKC hunt tests at the highest levels in North America.

Summer 2022

Using Creed as the stud for Demi was a recommendation from Clyde Vetter of SharpShooter Kennels. The Vetters have produced more versatile champions than any other german shorthair kennel in North America including our female Demi. Every attribute Creed has is very complimentary to Demi. Both dogs were incredibly easy to train and handle for test/trialing/hunting. Creed has qualified to run in the NAVHDA Invitational Fall 2022 where he hopes to attain the coveted title of Versatile Champion. This will no doubt be an exception litter.
